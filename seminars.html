  • Sept. 7th, Evan O'Connor, Stockholm Univ.

    Title: Core Collapse Supernovae and EOS thermal effects

    Abstract: Core-collapse supernovae (like mergers, the focus area of NP3M), are astrophysical sites rich with extreme physics and multimessenger signals. In this seminar I'll give a brief introduction to supernovae and the multimessenger signals expected from them. I'll also discuss in more detail our recent investigations into the impact of the thermal dependence in the nuclear equation of state in core-collapse supernova simulations, including the impact on the neutrino and gravitational wave signal, black hole formation properties, and the impact on potential ejecta from failed supernovae.
